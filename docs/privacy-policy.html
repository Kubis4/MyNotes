Privacy Policy for MyNotes

Last updated: July 19, 2026

MyNotes ("the app") is a personal note-taking and to-do list application developed by kub1sDev. This policy explains what data the app handles, why, and how you can control it.

Data stored on your device

Your notes, to-do lists, categories, and app settings are stored locally on your device in the app's private database. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable one of the optional features below.

Google Sign-In

Signing in with your Google account is required only for collaborative notes and cloud backup. The app receives your basic profile information (name, email address, profile picture) from Google to identify your account. We do not receive or store your Google password.

Collaborative notes (Firebase)

If you choose to share a note with another person, that note's content and the list of collaborators are stored in Google Firebase (Firestore) so it can sync in real time between participants. Notes that are not shared are never uploaded here. You can stop sharing a note at any time, which removes it from the shared collection.

The app also uses Firebase Crashlytics and Firebase Analytics to receive crash reports and basic, non-personally-identifying usage statistics (e.g. which screens are used), to help fix bugs and improve the app.

Google Drive backup

If you turn on backup, the app creates backup files in a folder it creates in your own Google Drive, using Google's restricted drive.file permission scope. This means the app can only see and manage the files it created itself - it cannot read, list, or access any other files already in your Google Drive. You can delete these backup files at any time from within the app or directly in Google Drive.

Biometric app lock

If you enable the optional fingerprint/face unlock for the app, authentication is handled entirely by your device's operating system. The app never receives, stores, or transmits your biometric data - it only receives a yes/no result from the OS.

Notifications

The app may show local reminder notifications you configure yourself. These are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device and are not sent from a server.

What we don't do

  • We do not sell or rent your data to third parties.
  • We do not show ads.
  • We do not read notes you have not chosen to share or back up.

Data deletion

You can delete individual notes, your entire local database (via your device's app settings), your cloud backups (from within the app or Google Drive), and your account's collaborative data by contacting us at the email below.
